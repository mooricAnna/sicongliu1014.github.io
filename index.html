I am a PhD candidate at School of Data Science and Engineering, Easy China Normal University, advised by Prof. Bin Yang and Asst. Prof. Yang Shu. I previously interned at Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Jian Cheng and Prof. Xi Sherly Zhang. I received my BS and MS degrees from the Nanjing University of Science and Technology.

My research lies at the intersection of machine learning, and reinforcement learning. I am interested in building scalable, robust, and open-source algorithms for decision-making that adapt to complex real world.
